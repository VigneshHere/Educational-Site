Ziya's Story

Our journey in transforming education since 2010

Founded in 2010, Ziya Academy began as a small coaching center with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and modern learning needs. What started with just 3 faculty members and 20 students has now grown into a premier educational institution serving hundreds of students annually.

2010

Foundation

Established as a neighborhood coaching center focusing on school curriculum

2014

Expansion

Added NIOS/IGNOU support programs and vocational training

2018

Tech Integration

Launched IT training and software development programs

2022

Current Campus

Moved to our modern facility with advanced computer labs and smart classrooms
